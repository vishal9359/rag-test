ACME Corp - Leave Calculation Rules

Pro-rating:
- Annual leave entitlement is pro-rated for partial years of service.
- Pro-rated formula: (months_worked / 12) * 24 days, rounded down to whole days.

Carry-over:
- Up to 5 unused leave days carry over to the next calendar year.
- Carried-over days expire at the end of Q1 of the new year.

Counting:
- Leave taken counts against the entitlement of the year in which it was taken.
- Maternity and paternity leave do NOT count against the 24-day annual leave.
- Sick leave beyond the 12-day cap requires HR approval and may be unpaid.
- Public holidays do not count as annual leave.
