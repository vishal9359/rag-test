ACME Corp Employee Handbook (Excerpt)

Leave Policy:
- All full-time employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
- Sick leave is capped at 12 days per year and requires a medical certificate beyond 2 consecutive days.
- Maternity leave is 26 weeks of fully paid leave.
- Paternity leave is 4 weeks of fully paid leave.

Remote Work:
- Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
- Fully remote roles are evaluated case-by-case by the People Operations team.

Reimbursement:
- Internet stipend: USD 40 / month for remote-eligible employees.
- Home-office one-time setup: USD 500, claimable within 90 days of joining.
