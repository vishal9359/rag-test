ACME RoboVac X9 - Product Specifications

Battery: 5200 mAh Li-ion, runtime up to 180 minutes on Eco mode.
Suction: 4000 Pa, three modes (Eco, Standard, Turbo).
Navigation: LiDAR + visual SLAM, supports multi-floor mapping (up to 4 floors).
Connectivity: Wi-Fi 2.4 GHz / 5 GHz, Bluetooth 5.2, Matter-over-Thread.
Dustbin: 600 ml, washable HEPA filter.
Warranty: 24 months limited warranty, extendable to 36 months with registration.
Price (MSRP): USD 649.

Known Limitations:
- Cannot climb thresholds higher than 22 mm.
- Carpet detection may be inaccurate on dark-colored shag rugs.
