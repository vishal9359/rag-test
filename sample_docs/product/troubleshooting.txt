ACME RoboVac X9 - Troubleshooting Guide

E01 - LiDAR obstructed:
  Wipe the top sensor dome with a microfiber cloth. Avoid alcohol-based cleaners.

E02 - Side brush stuck:
  Remove hair / fibers from the brush axle. Replace brush every 6 months.

E03 - Battery not charging:
  Check that the dock contacts are clean. If error persists for >24 hours,
  contact support. The battery is non-user-serviceable.

E04 - Map lost / re-mapping repeatedly:
  Ensure consistent lighting on each floor. Heavy seasonal furniture changes
  can trigger remapping; clear the saved map and re-run a full mapping pass.

Pairing:
  Open the ACME Home app, tap "Add device", and hold the dock button for 5
  seconds until the LED pulses. Use the 2.4 GHz network during pairing.
